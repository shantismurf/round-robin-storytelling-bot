## ASSEMBLED FAQ POST CONTENT (as it will appear in Discord)

**📖 Story Commands**
- `/story list` — Browse all stories on the server; filter by status or rating
- `/story join [id]` — Join a story
- `/story write [id]` — Submit your entry *(Quick Mode only)*
- `/story read [id]` — Read the story in Discord
- `/story edit [id]` — Edit one of your finalized entries
- `/story timeleft [id]` — See how much time is left in the current turn
- `/story ping [id]` — Ping all writers in a story
- `/story help` — Detailed guide with all writer options

**🗂️ Your Dashboard**
- `/mystory list` — See all your stories — active, paused, delayed, and closed
- `/mystory catchup [id]` — Read entries written since your last turn
- `/mystory manage [id]` — Update your settings, pass your turn, pause, or leave a story

**⚙️ Story Creator Commands**
- `/story manage [id]` — Edit story settings, manage turns and entries, pause or close

*Use `/story help` for detailed explanations of story modes, writer order, metadata, and more.*
